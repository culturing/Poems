### White Shade
<p style="margin:0; margin-top: -1.25rem">
  <em>
    <small><small>07 July 2019</small></small>
  </em>
</p>

A shade of white,\
not quite opaque,\
disturbs my sight.

It has no form,\
but haunts the night\
like one unsteadily born.

The ashes of a pyre\
lay where\
She was burned bright.

I do not see the Lady,\
and her absence \
haunts my sight.
