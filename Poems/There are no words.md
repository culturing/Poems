There are no words --  
no words, but only sounds  
with no meaning.  

Is there a balm?  
What is a balm?  

Nowhere are we to find solace.  
Nowhere are we to find others  
without lawlessness,  

And I know why the free bird sings,  
for lack of a cage,  
for lack of any air on which to glide.
