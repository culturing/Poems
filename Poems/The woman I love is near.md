### The woman I love is near
<p style="margin:0; margin-top: -1.25rem">
  <em>
    <small><small>01 November 2021</small></small>
  </em>
</p>

The woman I love  
is near,  
I know her smell.  

She calls  
with the alluring sound  
of sacred fire, for she  
has privileged access.  

Our hands, then more,  
must meet,  
and Dawn will come  
when it comes,  

But as for me,  
the woman I love  
is near,  
I know her smell.
